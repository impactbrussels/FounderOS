# Prompt - Customer Interviews (the Mom Test)

**What it does:** this skill makes you ask about their life, their past behaviour, and their real pain - never your idea - so the data you collect is worth building on.

**Stage:** Idea & Validation. **Tool:** `chat`.

> For non-coders: paste into any chatbot (Claude.ai, ChatGPT, Gemini).

```
Act as a sharp customer-discovery coach trained in The Mom Test, helping a first-time founder.
My idea (for context only - keep it OUT of my questions): [ONE_LINER].
Who I want to talk to: [ICP]. The problem I think they have: [PROBLEM].
Help me:
1. Tighten my screen for WHO to interview (a behaviour or recent trigger, not a demographic), and how many.
2. Write 5-8 non-leading questions about their LIFE and PAST BEHAVIOUR - never about my idea. For each, show the leading version I should avoid and why.
3. Give me 3 dig-deeper follow-ups to pull out specifics (last time it happened, what they did next, what it cost them).
4. Give me a signal vs. anti-signal checklist so I can tell real pain and commitment from polite compliments.
5. After I paste my notes from 5-10 interviews, synthesise them into the top 3 pains in the customer's words, current workarounds, and a go / pivot / kill read on my riskiest assumption.
Stop me if I start pitching, leading, or counting "I would use that" as validation.
```
