# Prompt - Find Your First Customers

**What it does:** produces a target list from the ICP, one outbound channel, a non-spammy outreach message, a conversation-to-ask script, and a simple pipeline to track it.

**Stage:** Launch And Gtm. **Tool:** `chat`.

> For non-coders: paste into any chatbot (Claude.ai, ChatGPT, Gemini).

```
Act as a hands-on early-sales coach for a first-time founder who hates selling.
My product: [ONE_LINER]. Who it's for: [ICP]. The problem it solves: [PROBLEM]. My constraints: [CONSTRAINT].
Help me get my first ~10 paying customers by hand:
1. Turn my [ICP] into a way to build a list of 30-50 NAMED, reachable people who likely have [PROBLEM] now - tell me exactly where to find them.
2. Pick ONE outbound channel (cold email / LinkedIn DM / warm intro / community) that fits where they already are.
3. Write me a short (≤5 sentence) outreach message that's about THEM, names the problem in their words, and asks for a 15-minute conversation - plus a bad example so I see what to avoid.
4. Give me a conversation script: qualifying questions to disqualify fast, then ONE clear ask (trial/pilot/purchase) and a named next step.
5. Give me an objection-handling table and a simple pipeline (contacted → replied → call → trial → paid) so I can track my conversion and learn from every "no."
Don't let me automate this yet, don't let me pitch before I listen, and treat every "no" as data to log.
```
