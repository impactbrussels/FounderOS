# Code of Conduct

FounderOS is built by founders, for founders. We want it to be a welcoming, useful, and
respectful place for everyone who contributes - whatever their background, experience level, or
the kind of company they're building.

## Our standard

We adopt the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
as our code of conduct. Please read it - it is short and clear.

In plain terms, we expect everyone to:

- **Be respectful and constructive.** Critique ideas, not people. Assume good intent.
- **Be welcoming.** First-time contributors and first-time founders are exactly who this project
  is for. No question is too basic.
- **Be honest and humble.** Cite your sources, flag your assumptions, and be open to review.
- **Keep it professional.** No harassment, demeaning comments, or personal attacks, in issues,
  pull requests, discussions, or any project space.

## Scope

This applies to all FounderOS spaces - the repository, issues, pull requests, discussions - and
to anyone representing the project in public.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, report it confidentially to the maintainers
via [impact.brussels](https://impact.brussels). Reports will be reviewed and handled with
discretion. Maintainers may remove content, decline contributions, or ban contributors who
violate this code.
